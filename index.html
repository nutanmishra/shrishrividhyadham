A Few Words About Us

Hindu Faith is a non-profit organization established with the goal of educating and supporting English-speaking Hindus.

We educate the public about Hinduism, speak out about issues affecting Hindus worldwide, and build bridges with institutions and individuals whose work aligns with our objectives and global goals of our organization.

Learn more
Learn more

Education

One of our goals is to educate Hindus worldwide by promoting a balanced understanding of Hinduism as a lived tradition.

Policy

We see one of our tasks in advocating for policies that enhance the well-being and secure the human rights of Hindus globally.

Community

Hindu Faith aims to enhance the well-being, safety, and security of Hindu communities & institutions and build a culture of advocacy.

Our Services

Hindu Meetings

We organize regular meetings of Hinduists in the US and support various public discussions that are related to Hinduism and its development.

Hindu Forums

Our organization members take part and organize numerous Hindu forums that unite Hindus from all over the world and establish international connections.

Esoteric Hinduism Events

Hindu Faith often acts as an organizer of various Esoteric Hinduism events all over the US and Europe. We aim to popularize this side of Hinduism as well.

Educational Services

Hindu Faith provides a wide variety of educational services by providing necessary assistance in teaching Hinduism in schools and colleges.

Organization of Festivals

Annually, we organize up to 3 Hinduism Festivals: Holi, Diwali, and Dussehra. They attract the attention of Non-Hindus to our religion.

Charitable Help

Our organization fully supports all kinds of charity by providing help to members of Hinduism community through giving, gifting, sharing and caring for others.
